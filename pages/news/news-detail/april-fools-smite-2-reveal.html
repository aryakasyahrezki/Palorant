GAME UPDATES APRIL 1, 2025

April Fools: Smite 2 Reveal

Yup, it's happening. The best skin is back and better than ever. Today we're revealing SMITE 2—the ultimate April Fools' joke that's actually real (for today only).

If you log in between the following times, you’ll be granted a free Blue Smite Buddy.
Separately, we will grant a free Gold Smite Buddy under certain conditions.

What's New?

We've taken everything you loved about the original SMITE skin and dialed it up to 11:

  • New VFX that actually blinds opponents (temporarily)
  • Custom hit sounds that play "BONK" on headshots
  • Ragdoll physics when eliminating enemies
  • Secret golden variant after 10 kills in a match

Availability

SMITE 2 will be available in the store today only (April 1) for 1,000 PP. After today, it's gone forever—or at least until next April Fools'.

"We wanted to create something that would make players do a double-take when they see it in-game. SMITE 2 is our love letter to the meme that started it all."
- Alex "MEME LORD" Chang, Weapon Skins Team
