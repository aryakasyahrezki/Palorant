PALORANT x Arcane

The wait is over! PALORANT is proud to present the Arcane Season 2 Collector's Set - a Limited Edition bundle of weapons and accessories available only during the Arcane Season 2 premiere event, in your shop from November 7 through December 5.

The complete Arcane Season 2 Collector's Set featuring Jinx and Vi's iconic weapons

What's in the Bundle

This exclusive collection brings Runeterra's most iconic weapons to PALORANT:

  • Arcane Vandal: An all-new Vandal skin with unique VFX, audio, animations, and an explosive Finisher inspired by Jinx's chaotic style
  • Arcane Gauntlets: A revolutionary melee weapon based on Vi's Hextech gloves with custom animations and sound effects
  • "Arcane Sisters" Player Card: Showcasing Jinx and Vi's complicated relationship
  • "Arcane Enforcer" Gun Buddy: A miniature Hextech core accessory
  • "BLAH" Spray: Jinx's iconic graffiti tag from the Undercity
“Arcane Sisters” Player Card

Twitch Drops & Rewards

Don't miss these additional ways to earn Arcane-themed rewards:

"We wanted to create something special for fans of both PALORANT and Arcane. The Collector's Set captures the essence of these beloved characters while staying true to PALORANT's competitive integrity."

Watch Arcane Season 2 on Twitch between November 8-23 to earn exclusive drops:

  • 1 hour watched: Special Jinx player card
  • 30 minutes watched: Vi player card
  • Tune in November 23: Exclusive finisher effect for Arcane weapons

Availability & Pricing

The Arcane Collector's Set will be available as a complete bundle for 2,380 PP from November 7 (2:00 PM PT) through December 5 (2:00 PM PT). After this period, these items won't return to the PALORANT Store or Night Market.
