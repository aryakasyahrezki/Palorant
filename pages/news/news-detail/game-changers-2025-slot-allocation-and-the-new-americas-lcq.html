ESPORTS FEBRUARY 25, 2025

Game Changers 2025

Today we're announcing the initial details of the 2025 Game Changers Championship and the all-new Americas LCQ! This year's program features expanded opportunities for women and marginalized genders to compete at the highest levels of PALORANT esports.

The new Game Changers 2025 branding featuring the updated PALORANT visual identity

Championship Slot Allocation

The 2025 Championship will feature 16 teams from across the world:

  • North America: 4 slots
  • Europe: 3 slots
  • Brazil: 2 slots
  • Latin America: 2 slots
  • Asia-Pacific: 3 slots
  • East Asia: 2 slots

Americas Last Chance Qualifier

New for 2025, we're introducing the Americas LCQ - a tournament that will give one additional team from North America, Brazil, and Latin America a final chance to qualify for the Championship:

  • Dates: August 15-18, 2025
  • Location: São Paulo, Brazil
  • Format: Double elimination bracket
  • Prize Pool: $50,000 USD

Program Enhancements

This year's Game Changers program includes several improvements:

"We're committed to building sustainable pathways for women in esports. The 2025 Game Changers season features enhanced support systems, including mental health resources and career development programs for all participants."
- Jamie "GiotMinerva" Metzner, PALORANT Esports Diversity Lead

Additional enhancements include increased production value for regional tournaments and expanded broadcast talent development opportunities.
