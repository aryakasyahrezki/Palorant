COMMUNITY JANUARY 17, 2025

Support LA Wildfire Relief

As unprecedented wildfires continue to impact Southern California, PALORANT is launching special in-game initiatives to support relief efforts. Starting January 22, the EX.O Collection will become a Give Back Bundle, with all proceeds going to wildfire recovery through the GIOT Social Impact Fund :cite[1]:cite[3].

The EX.O Collection will support wildfire relief when purchased between January 22-30

Supporting Our Community

The fires have directly impacted our Los Angeles-based teams, with some GIOT employees losing homes and many others evacuated. Our priority remains supporting these team members through temporary housing, emergency stipends, and advisory services while maintaining PALORANT operations :cite[1]:cite[4].

"When disaster strikes this close to home, we must respond as both a company and a community. The EX.O Give Back Bundle lets PALORANT players worldwide support recovery efforts while getting premium content."
- Marcus "Sentinel" Lee, PALORANT Social Impact Director
The full EX.O Collection available as a Give Back Bundle until January 30

Fundraiser Details

From January 22 at 2 PM PT through January 30, PALORANT will donate all proceeds from:

  • The complete EX.O Collection bundle (2,500 PP)
  • Individual EX.O weapon skins (1,075 PP each)
  • EX.O Gun Buddy (300 PP)
  • EX.O Player Card (200 PP)

Funds will support organizations including the California Fire Foundation, Los Angeles Fire Department Foundation, and International Medical Corps through the GIOT Social Impact Fund :cite[1]:cite[5].

Long-Term Commitment

Beyond this fundraiser, GIOT has provided direct aid including:

  • Emergency generators to local fire stations
  • 400+ meals for first responders via World Central Kitchen
  • Triple-match donations for employee contributions

"Recovery doesn't end when fires are extinguished," said Lee. "We're committed to supporting our community through the rebuilding process and strengthening disaster preparedness globally." :cite[1]
