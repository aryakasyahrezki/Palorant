ESPORTS MARCH 19, 2025

Masters Toronto

We're thrilled to announce that tickets for PALORANT Masters Toronto will be available starting March 25 at 7 AM PT / 10 AM ET! The event will take place at the Coca-Cola Coliseum from June 10-23, marking our first international LAN event in Canada.

Coca-Cola Coliseum will host PALORANT Masters Toronto

Ticket Information

Tickets will be sold through Ticketmaster with the following pricing structure:

  • General Admission: $35 per day
  • Premium Seating: $75 per day (includes exclusive merch)
  • Weekend Pass: $120 (access to all weekend matches)
  • Full Event Pass: $300 (access to all days)

Event Schedule

The tournament will follow this format:

  • Group Stage: June 10-15 (Double Elimination)
  • Playoffs: June 17-20 (Single Elimination)
  • Finals Weekend: June 22-23 (Grand Finals)

Health & Safety

We're working closely with venue officials to implement comprehensive health measures:

"The safety of our players and fans is our top priority. We'll follow all local guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone attending PALORANT Masters Toronto."
- Dr. Sarah Chen, Event Safety Coordinator

All attendees will be required to show proof of vaccination or a negative COVID-19 test taken within 48 hours of entry.
