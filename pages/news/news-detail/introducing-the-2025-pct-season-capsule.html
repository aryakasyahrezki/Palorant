PATCH NOTES JANUARY 27, 2025

Introducing the 2025 PCT Season Capsule!

Today we're introducing the all-new PCT 2025 Season Capsule - a celebration of the competitive season and a revolutionary way to represent your region and home league in PALORANT! This season features our largest esports collection ever, giving players unprecedented options to support their favorite teams and leagues :cite[1]:cite[6].

The complete 2025 PCT Season Capsule collection featuring the Karambit melee weapon and regional items

What's in the 2025 Season Capsule?

The 2025 collection brings these exclusive items to PALORANT:

  • PCT Karambit: A sleek new melee weapon with five chromas representing the four International Leagues and PCT theme. Top fraggers unlock a special glow effect at Level 3 :cite[1]:cite[7]
  • 4 Regional Player Cards: Animated designs celebrating each International League's identity
  • 4 Gun Buddies: Reactive charms representing each region that activate when fired
  • Exclusive Finisher: Regional-themed celebration for match victories

Supporting Teams Equally

While Team Capsules let you rep specific squads, the Season Capsule showcases pride in your entire region. Most importantly, proceeds are split equally among all PCT teams - creating a sustainable ecosystem where every organization benefits :cite[1]:cite[8].

"We noticed some teams benefited significantly more than others from individual capsules last year. The Season Capsule raises the baseline for all competitors, ensuring every team gets support regardless of tournament performance."
- Jamie "GIOT" Chen, PALORANT Esports Director

In 2024, fans generated over $44 million USD for partnered teams through esports cosmetics. The Season Capsule aims to surpass this by offering collectors premium items while maintaining competitive integrity :cite[1].

Availability & Pricing

The 2025 Season Capsule launches globally on February 6 at 2 PM PT in the in-game store, remaining available until Masters Bangkok concludes. Priced at 2,500 PP, this limited-time collection won't return to the store or Night Market :cite[1]:cite[6].

Regional activation events will occur throughout the season, with special viewership rewards during PCT broadcasts. Fans can earn additional gun buddies and player cards by watching official matches :cite[5].
